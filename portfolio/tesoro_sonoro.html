About Tesoro Sonoro

Tesoro Sonoro is a project launched under Images in Action, a non-profit that I co-founded back in 2008.

Project goals

Tesoro Sonoro which translated means “Audio Treasures” is a project that audio-records and shares the stories of the people of Buenos Aires. The goal is to contribute to the creation of the intangible memory of the city by creating a virtual exchange of stories and preserve them for future generations. The radio show “This American Life” has had a large influence since we found in radio documentaries a way to spread stories combining the power of the internet and strengthening the imagination of the audience.

Process: methods and techniques

  • In-depth interviews and audio recording to persons that migrate to Buenos Aires in different periods of time and from different countries.

Highlights:

  • Check the site to listen to some stories.
  • Tesoro Sonoro was declared of Cultural Interest by the Government of Buenos Aires.
