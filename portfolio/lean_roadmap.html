Background & Problem

In 2011 co-founded an internet startup named Chef Surfing. Since that time I have been surrounded by entrepreneurs due mostly to Chef Surfing's participation in Startup Chile. During this time I have learned about "Lean methodology" as it applies to startups. I have observed that while many entrepreneurs are following the methodology, most get stuck in the customer development process. Specifically they have trouble understanding and executing on the idea "get out of the building". Unfortunately understanding customers and iterating based on market feedback to reach success while executing a new innovation is not as common as I thought it could be. Simply put: lean practitioners want to be able to build a feedback loop by crafting effective experiments that lead to breakthrough insights but they just don't know how.
